How It Works

Feature Extraction

Analyzes 90-day transaction history using Deque sliding window to extract behavioral patterns.

Credit Score

CIBIL-weighted formula: Payment History (35%), Utilization (30%), DTI (20%), Stability (10%), Salary (5%).

Approval Decision

Decision tree with hard reject rules and tier assignment (A/B/C) based on score and profile.

Risk Assessment

Flags identify concerning patterns: missed payments, high utilization, irregular income, volatility.

Approval Criteria

Tier A (750-900)

  • Score ≥ 750
  • Min Missed Payments: 0
  • Credit Limit: 3× income (max Rs.10L)
  • Interest Rate: 18% p.a.

Tier B (600-749)

  • Score 600-749
  • Max Missed Payments: 2
  • Credit Limit: 1.5× income (max Rs.5L)
  • Interest Rate: 24% p.a.

Tier C (500-599)

  • Score 500-599
  • Max Missed Payments: 3
  • Credit Limit: 0.5× income (max Rs.2L)
  • Interest Rate: 36% p.a.

Rejection (<500)

  • Score < 500
  • DTI > 55%
  • Missed Payments > 3
  • Utilization > 90%
